On April 3, 2024, Starship Entertainment announced that Ive would be releasing their second extended play titled Ive Switch on April 29. On April 20, the track listing was released with "Heya" and "Accendio" announced as the lead singles. A day later, a motion teaser video was released,followed by the music video teaser on April 24. On April 28, the highlight medley teaser video was released. The song was released alongside its music video and the extended play on April 29. "Heya" was written by Lee Seu-ran, Exy, and Sohlhee, composed and arranged by Ryan S. Jhun, Dem Jointz, and Ido Nadjar, with Kloe Latimer, Jack Brady, and Jordan Roman participating in the composition, and The Wavys participating in the arrangement. Described as a hip hop song with lyrics about "folk tale"."Heya" was composed in the key of F minor, with a tempo of 92 beats per minute.

In South Korea, "Heya" debuted at number 11 on the Billboard South Korea Songs in the chart issue dated May 11, 2024. Following the release of Ive Switch, on April 29, 2024, Ive held a live event on YouTube aimed at introducing the extended play and its tracks, including "Heya", and connecting with their fanbase.
